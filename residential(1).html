Scope of Service

What's Included

01

Needs Assessment

A detailed consultation to define your property criteria, lifestyle needs, budget, and investment objectives. We take time to understand what matters most to you before presenting any options.

02

Property Sourcing

Access to on-market listings and exclusive off-market opportunities across all premium Dubai neighbourhoods. Our network gives you access to properties before they reach the open market.

03

Site Visits & Shortlisting

Curated selection of properties with accompanied site visits and detailed comparative analysis. We provide written assessments of each property's strengths, risks, and investment potential.

04

Price Negotiation

Expert negotiation to secure the most competitive terms and price on your behalf. Our advisors have deep knowledge of market values and leverage relationships to achieve the best outcome.

05

Legal & Due Diligence

Coordination with trusted legal partners to verify title deeds, ownership structure, and regulatory compliance. We ensure your investment is fully protected before any commitment is made.

06

Handover Support

Full support through the transfer process at the Dubai Land Department, including snagging inspection and key handover. We stay with you until the property is officially yours.

How We Work

Our Process

A transparent, step-by-step approach designed to give you clarity and confidence at every stage.

01

Discovery Call

We begin with a confidential consultation to understand your requirements, budget, lifestyle needs, and timeline. This call shapes every step that follows.

~1 hour · Remote or in-person
02

Market Briefing

Our team presents a tailored market overview, pricing benchmarks, and a curated selection of properties matching your criteria — including exclusive off-market options.

2–5 days

Viewings & Evaluation

We organise accompanied property visits and provide detailed written assessments for each shortlisted property, including condition notes and investment considerations.

1–2 weeks
04

Offer & Negotiation

Luxenor manages all negotiations with sellers and agents, leveraging market knowledge and relationships to secure the best possible price and terms for you.

3–7 days
05

We oversee every step of the legal transfer, payment, and DLD registration process. Our team is present at handover to ensure the property meets the agreed standard.

2–4 weeks · Full legal support
