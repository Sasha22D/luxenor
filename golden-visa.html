Overview

Residency Through Real Estate

The UAE Golden Visa programme offers long-term residency to investors who purchase property above defined thresholds. Luxenor combines real estate expertise with in-depth knowledge of UAE immigration requirements to guide you through both the property acquisition and the visa application process.

We ensure your investment is structured correctly from the outset — selecting eligible properties, navigating RERA and ICA requirements, and coordinating with legal and immigration specialists — so your pathway to UAE residency is as smooth and efficient as possible.
